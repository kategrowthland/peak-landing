When to use which.

Use Amberdata when you need:

  • →Broad historical coverage across markets, blockchain (ETH-centric), and DeFi
  • →AI-driven analytics and research workflow features
  • →Ethereum-focused DeFi protocol analytics
  • →RWA and tokenized asset data infrastructure
  • →Multi-year historical data depth for backtesting on ETH chains

Use Birdeye Peak when you need:

  • →Real-time Solana DEX liquidity depth for institutional-size allocation
  • →Pre-trade on-chain due diligence before token entry
  • →Smart money wallet tracking and institutional accumulation signals
  • →Liquidity migration detection with advance warning signals
  • →On-chain evidence layer for IC reports on Solana positions

These platforms serve different coverage needs. Amberdata brings breadth across the digital asset landscape with strong ETH-chain depth. Peak brings Solana on-chain intelligence that Amberdata's infrastructure wasn't built to cover. Funds running Solana exposure benefit from running both.
