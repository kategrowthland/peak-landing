What you're missing today

Three on-chain failure modes that surface after entry — not before.

Failure Mode 01

Thin liquidity at size

The token looks liquid on a CEX screen. At $5M+ trade size, on-chain DEX pool depth tells a different story. You discover the slippage problem at execution, not at IC.

Avg slippage at $10M: 2.3% undisclosed
Failure Mode 02

Wallet concentration overhang

Top-10 wallets holding 40%+ creates a structurally fragile token. One team wallet rotation changes the price dynamic. On-chain holder analysis before entry reveals this risk.

38% avg top-10 concentration in Solana mid-caps
Failure Mode 03

Liquidity migration post-entry

TVL rotates from your entry pool to a competing venue. Your exit liquidity shrinks without warning. Peak monitors pool migration continuously and alerts before it affects your position.

Avg migration window: 6–18 hours advance signal

The framework

Peak's 5-point pre-trade on-chain audit.

A structured checklist your investment team runs before any institutional-size token entry. Each signal is verifiable on-chain — exportable for IC presentation.

01

Liquidity depth audit

Measure true DEX pool depth at your target entry and exit size. Query bid/ask depth at $1M, $5M, $10M+ trade sizes across all Solana pools. Compare slippage curves against execution threshold.

Source: Peak real-time DEX pool API · Updated 250ms

Key questions

→Can we enter/exit $X at under 1% slippage?
→Is depth concentrated in one pool or distributed?
→Has depth been stable or volatile over 30 days?
02

Holder concentration analysis

Map the wallet distribution landscape. Identify team wallets, VC lockups, and anonymous whale cohorts. Score concentration risk using top-10, top-50, and top-100 holder percentages.

Source: Peak on-chain wallet intelligence · 5M+ tokens

Key questions

→What % do top-10 wallets control?
→Are team wallets still accumulating or distributing?
→Has concentration changed significantly in 90 days?
03

Smart money signal check

Identify whether historically profitable wallets and institutional cohorts are accumulating, holding, or distributing. Detect whale net flow divergence vs. retail wallet behavior.

Source: Peak smart money wallet database · Solana + 7 chains

Key questions

→Are smart wallets net buyers or net sellers?
→Has institutional flow accelerated in the last 14 days?
→Is the accumulation pattern consistent with prior rallies?
04

Unlock event calendar

Surface all scheduled and estimated token unlock events within your investment horizon. Model the supply impact on liquidity and price at current pool depth.

Source: Peak unlock database · On-chain verified

Key questions

→What % of supply unlocks in the next 30/90/180 days?
→Are unlock recipients known (team, investors, ecosystem)?
→Does unlock timing overlap with our target hold period?
05

Pool migration risk assessment

Evaluate whether primary liquidity pools are structurally stable or showing signs of LP migration. Identify historical migration events and estimate fragility of current pool configuration.

Source: Peak liquidity migration signals · Real-time

Key questions

→Is TVL in primary pools growing or declining?
→Are LPs concentrated (fragile) or distributed (stable)?
→Has there been a migration event in the prior 60 days?
